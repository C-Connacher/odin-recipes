Mexican Rice

Authentic Mexican Rice Family Recipe

Authentic recipe passed down from my abuelita. A perfect complimentary dish for any meal, breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Roma Tomatoes (Quartered)
  • 1/4 White Onion (Halved)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (Smahed)
  • 1 Cup White Rice
  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Caldo de Pollo (Chicken Boullion)
  • 1/4 Cup Water

Steps:

  1. Prepare ingredients and place in blender. For a chunk finish, reserve 1/2 roma tomato).
  2. Heat 1 Tbsp oil. Lightly brown 1 Cup rice, stirring often and making sure not to burn.
  3. Blend mixture until smooth consistency. For chunky finish, add 1/2 Roma tomato and blend until desired consistency.
  4. Make sure mixture is equal to 2 cups, add water as needed.
  5. Add mixture to rice, stir to combine. Once boiling, reduce heat and cover (Do not mix rice until complete).
  6. Check rice after 20min. If watery, leave rice on for 5min. Otherwise, stir to combine and serve immediately.
