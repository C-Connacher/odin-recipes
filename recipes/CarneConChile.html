Carne Con Chile Recipe

A classic dish that's quick and easy to make! Serve with rice, beans, or eat it on it's own!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs flank steak (1 in. pieces)
  • 3 Jalapeno Peppers (stems and seeds removed)
  • 4 Roma Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 White Onion (julienned)
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in nonstick skillet. Add chicken, salt, and pepper, and cook until lightly browned.
  2. Add remaining oil, jalapenos, tomatoes, onion, and garlic and reduce heat to medium. Cook for 5 min, stirring frequently.
  3. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low until a light sauce begins to form.
  4. Serve immediately with fresh tortillas, rice, or any other dish!
